Routines written while counting galaxies. All routines may be
downloaded from <a href="ajs.tar.gz">ajs.tar.gz</a> or via <a href="https://github.com/anthonyjsmith/ajs" target="_top">GitHub</a>. Questions,
comments, etc - send them this way: <a href="mailto:anthonysmith80@gmail.com">anthonysmith80@gmail.com</a>.

<li>One or two things from <a href="http://michaelgalloy.com/">Michael Galloy</a>
